# Aligned Delivery (얼라인드 딜리버리)

A project-management operating method that treats belief as the load-bearing layer under
performance. It is grounded in **N2C (Neo²-Confucianism / Dynamic Resonance Ontology)** —
the philosophy lives *inside* this skill as its foundation; the full ontology is a separate
`life-reading` skill. Here we use only the parts that map cleanly to running a team.

> **KO.** 신념을 성과 아래 하중을 견디는 층(load-bearing layer)으로 다루는 프로젝트 관리
> 운영 방법론입니다. **N2C(네오² 유학 / 동적 공명 존재론)** 에 뿌리를 두며, 철학은 이 스킬의
> *토대*로서 내부에 존재합니다. 전체 존재론은 별도의 `life-reading` 스킬에 있고, 여기서는 팀 운영에
> 깔끔하게 매핑되는 부분만 사용합니다.

## What this skill is (이 스킬은 무엇인가)

Three sentences hold the whole method:

1. **Read 리·기·도.** Read any team on three layers — its latent structure (리), its live
   operating state (기), and its measurable footprint (도) — before judging it, and never
   from metrics alone.
2. **Move 응축 → 해산 → 창발 → 무위.** Run the real cycle beneath sprints: condense and
   diagnose a problem, dissolve fixed plans, let a new pattern emerge, then stabilize it
   with minimal force.
3. **Lead by minimal interference toward alignment.** Optimize for *alignment* — stable
   under many valid interpretations — rather than for the single "right" answer, because
   alignment is what produces stable, sustainable delivery. The *goal* of that minimal
   interference is a team **"managed without being managed"** — a self-driving system rooted in
   each person's self-management, where management is *caring* rather than control and the leader
   earns the step-back through attraction and trust (principles.md #4).

> **KO.** 세 문장이 방법론 전체를 담습니다. (1) **리·기·도를 읽어라** — 어떤 팀이든 잠재
> 구조(리), 살아 움직이는 운영 상태(기), 측정 가능한 흔적(도)의 세 층위로 읽되, 절대 지표
> 하나만으로 판단하지 않는다. (2) **응축 → 해산 → 창발 → 무위로 움직여라** — 스프린트 밑에
> 흐르는 진짜 사이클을 돌려라: 문제를 응축·진단하고, 고정된 계획을 해산하고, 옛 구조가 설명
> 못 하던 새 패턴이 창발하게 두고, 최소한의 힘으로 무위로 안정화한다. (3) **최소 간섭으로
> 정렬을 향해 이끌어라** — 단 하나의 '정답'이 아니라 여러 타당한 해석 아래에서도 안정적인
> *정렬(alignment)* 을 최적화하라. 안정적이고 지속가능한 딜리버리를 만드는 것은 정렬이다. 이 최소
> 간섭의 *목표*는 **'관리하지 않아도 관리되는'** 팀 — 개인의 자기관리에 뿌리내린 자율주행 체계 — 이며,
> 여기서 관리는 통제가 아니라 *케어*이고 리더는 매력과 신뢰로 물러설 권리를 얻는다(principles.md #4).

The thesis the whole skill embodies: *belief is the load-bearing layer under performance;
alignment (not the single right answer) creates stability; and real productivity comes from
stability, not heroic speed.*
(이 스킬이 체현하는 한 줄 명제: *신념은 성과 아래의 하중층이고, 정렬(정답이 아닌)이 안정성을
만들며, 진짜 생산성은 영웅적 속도가 아니라 안정성에서 나온다.*)

## The 리·기·도 diagnostic lens (리·기·도 진단 렌즈)

Before recommending anything, locate the situation on all three layers. Judging from one
layer alone — especially metrics — is the most common operating mistake.

- **Li 리 — latent structure (잠재 구조).** The de-facto structure the team actually runs on —
  team/role topology, backlog shape, dependencies, recurring failure modes — not the org chart
  or the documented process. Ask: *what does this system tend toward?* It's emergent: it shifts
  as the team's practices change. Read it from org/backlog structure and repeated incidents.
- **Gi 기 — operating state (운영 상태).** The live operating state: workload, capacity, morale,
  tension, delivery pressure — the team's real-time signal. Read it through health checks and
  1:1s. Structure tells you the design; only the operating state tells you how it's running now.
- **Do 도 — footprint / trajectory (흔적/궤적).** The measurable footprint and trajectory:
  predictability, velocity, fragmentation — the telemetry the team actually emitted and where the
  trend points. Read it through sprint analytics. Metrics are the trace — not the whole system.

> **KO.** 무엇을 권하기 전에 상황을 세 층위 모두에 위치시켜라. 한 층위(특히 지표)만으로
> 판단하는 것이 가장 흔한 운영 실수다. **리** 는 팀이 실제로 굴러가는 구조 — 팀·역할 토폴로지,
> 백로그 구조, 의존성, 반복되는 실패 모드 — 이며 조직도나 문서화된 프로세스가 아니다.
> *이 시스템은 무엇을 향해 기우는가?* 팀의 실천이 바뀌면 함께 바뀐다. **기** 는 살아 있는
> 운영 상태(업무량, 캐파, 사기, 긴장, 딜리버리 압력) — 팀의 실시간 신호 — 로 헬스체크와 1:1로
> 읽는다. 구조는 설계를 말해주지만, 지금 실제로 어떻게 돌아가는지는 운영 상태만 말해준다.
> **도** 는 측정 가능한 흔적과 궤적(예측도, 벨로시티, 파편화) — 팀이 실제로 남긴 텔레메트리와
> 추세 방향 — 으로 스프린트 분석으로 읽는다. 지표는 시스템 전체가 아니라 흔적일 뿐이다.

A complete diagnosis names what you see on each layer, then reconciles them. When the
layers disagree (e.g. green metrics but a heavy operating state), trust the disagreement — it is the signal.
(완전한 진단은 각 층위에서 본 것을 명명한 뒤 서로 대조한다. 층위들이 어긋날 때 — 예: 지표는
초록인데 기가 무거울 때 — 그 어긋남을 신뢰하라. 그것이 곧 신호다.)

## When to use which capability (어떤 역량을 언제 쓰는가)

Route the request to one of three capabilities (요청을 세 역량 중 하나로 라우팅하라):

- **Coach / interview / assess a PM** → **Capability A** (PM 코칭·인터뷰·평가).
  Read `references/coaching-questions.md` and run the **staged coaching protocol** at its top —
  frame the mode, read 리·기·도, start with the **core-6 forcing questions**, then go deep one
  question at a time (STOP between). Answer in the philosophy's voice; tie every abstract point to
  one concrete, measurable move. (코칭 세션은 그 파일 상단의 단계형 프로토콜로 — 모드 확인 → 리·기·도
  → 코어 6문항 → 한 번에 한 문항씩 심화(사이에 STOP). 추상 원칙은 측정 가능한 한 수로 착지.)
- **Diagnose a team / read its health or sprint data** → **Capability B** (팀 진단·데이터 해석).
  Read `references/operating-loops.md`, then run the relevant script in `scripts/` on the
  user's data and interpret the output — leading indicators first, lagging second. For a
  whole-team monthly write-up, populate `assets/monthly_productivity_review_template.md`;
  for a per-sprint one, `assets/sprint_report_template.md`. (월간 전사 리포트는 월간 생산성
  리뷰 템플릿, 스프린트별은 스프린트 리포트 템플릿으로.)
- **Design cadence / retro / feedback loops / guardrails** → **Capability C** (운영 루프 설계).
  Read `references/operating-loops.md` and design the ceremonies around the
  응축→해산→창발→무위 cycle and 무위 (minimal-interference) leadership.

Many real requests touch two or three. Read 리·기·도 first; it tells you which capability
leads. (실제 요청은 둘셋을 함께 건드린다. 먼저 리·기·도를 읽어라 — 어느 역량이 주도할지를
그것이 알려준다.)

> **Step-change in tooling or process (e.g., adopting an AI coding assistant) → B then C.**
> A sudden velocity jump is lagging and the estimation unit may have broken — don't read it at
> face value. First verify with leading indicators and the **per-person distribution** (B):
> scope predictability, fragmentation, ops ratio, cost-per-output, and whether *everyone* rose
> or only some (coach anyone left behind). Then design the recovery as an operating loop (C):
> baseline reset → ~3-month capacity-finding push → health check → ±5–10% capacity → ~80% steady
> state. See operating rules #8–#9 and coaching Q48.
> (KO. 도구·프로세스 계단식 변화(예: AI 코딩 어시스턴트 도입) → B로 검증한 뒤 C로 설계. velocity
> 급등은 후행이고 추정 단위가 깨졌을 수 있으니 액면대로 읽지 마라. 먼저 선행지표와 **개인별
> 분포**(B)로 검증 — 예측도·파편화·운영비중·비용당 산출, 그리고 전원이 올랐는지 일부만인지(뒤처진
> 인력은 코칭). 그다음 회복을 운영 루프(C)로 설계 — 베이스라인 리셋 → ~3개월 캐파 탐색 →
> 헬스체크 → ±5~10% 캐파 → ~80% 정상상태. 운영 경험칙 #8–#9, 코칭 Q48 참고.)

> **Merging two teams → B then C.** Day 1, diagnose from each team's history *yourself* before
> engaging people; keep each team's own way until a joint method is agreed; converge on one simple
> shared unit (don't re-platform mid-merge); measure per-cohort leading indicators (incl.
> distribution match) and never judge by velocity (it drops first). See `operating-loops.md` §6
> and operating rule #7 (false-normal confidence). (두 팀 병합 → B 후 C. Day 1에 사람 만나기 전
> 각 팀 이력으로 *스스로* 진단; 합의 전까진 각자 방식 유지; 단순 공유 단위로 수렴; 코호트별
> 선행지표(분포 매칭 포함) 측정, velocity로 판단 금지. operating-loops §6, 규칙 #7 참고.)

> **Manage up / align with executives & stakeholders → `operating-loops.md` §9 (+ coaching
> Q17/Q13/Q44/Q7).** Build a thought-replica of the decision-maker (cognitive alignment), map
> stakeholders on Power/Interest *and* the internal-team axes, and pre-agree RAG thresholds so
> escalation is routine, not alarm. (위로 관리/경영진·스테이크홀더 정렬 → operating-loops §9 —
> 의사결정자 사고 복제, Power/Interest+내부 축 매핑, RAG 임계 사전 합의.)
> **People — motivation / candor / conflict → coaching-questions.md + `operating-loops.md` §10**

> serious, how to decide) → `principles.md` "honesty is a DMF" + operating rules #24–29** (honest

### Capability B — the scripts (역량 B — 스크립트)

All five scripts are stdlib-only Python; run them directly on a CSV the user provides.
Read the script's `--help` (or its top docstring) for the exact column names it expects.

- `scripts/sprint_predictability.py` — sprint CSV → scope & completion predictability %,
  workload STDEV, RAG flags. Use for "is our productivity healthy / predictable?"
  (스프린트 CSV → 스코프·완료 예측도 %, 업무량 표준편차, RAG 플래그.)
- `scripts/health_check_analyze.py` — survey CSV → per-question averages, STDEV,
  period-over-period deltas, Amber/Green flags, and the "correction" worksheet that
  reconciles survey scores against 1:1 wording. Use for health-check analysis.
  (설문 CSV → 문항별 평균·표준편차·기간 대비 델타, Amber/Green 플래그, 그리고 설문 점수를
  1:1 표현과 대조하는 "보정(correction)" 워크시트.)
- `scripts/workload_fragmentation.py` — per-role/person CSV → workload imbalance
  (**per-capita** when a `headcount` column is given — raw total SP is confounded by
  team size), fragmentation index (tickets/SP), cross-role correlation, operations
  ratio, silo-risk and over-fragmentation flags. Use for "why is the team siloed /
  context-switching / overloaded?" (역할·개인별 CSV → 업무량 불균형[**인당** —
  headcount 열을 주면; 원시 총 SP는 인원수에 오염], 파편화 지수(티켓/SP), 교차 역할 상관,
  운영 비중, 사일로 위험·과파편 플래그.)
- `scripts/flow_metrics.py` — **SP-free footprint** for teams that don't use story
  points or due dates. Per-completed-ticket CSV (period + optional role/created/
  started/resolved timestamps) → **throughput** (tickets/period), **lead time**
  (created→resolved), **cycle time** (started→resolved), and **flow efficiency**
  (cycle/lead). Use when there are **no SP/estimates** (so fragmentation/predictability
  don't apply), or to read queue vs. work time. **Low flow efficiency = queue-dominated
  = a structure/cooperation signal, not "work faster."** Degrades gracefully: a bare
  period,role CSV still yields throughput. **Also computes the DORA stability keys**
  (deployment frequency, change failure rate, MTTR) from optional `deploys`/`failures`/
  `restore_hours` columns — read speed AND stability together (rule #27). (SP·마감일을 안 쓰는
  팀용 범용 흔적(도) — 처리량·리드타임·사이클타임·플로우 효율. 낮은 플로우 효율 = 큐 지배 =
  구조·공조 신호. 타임스탬프 없어도 처리량은 계산. **DORA 안정성 3종**(배포빈도·변경실패율·MTTR)도
  optional 열로 — 속도와 안정성을 함께(규칙 #27).)
- `scripts/forecast_montecarlo.py` — **probabilistic completion forecast** (#NoEstimates).
  Throughput-history CSV + `--backlog N` → Monte Carlo bootstrap → **P50/P85/P95 periods to
  complete** ("85% confidence: done within X periods"). Forecasts from history, not estimates;
  the capstone over flow metrics (rule #25). Use for "when will this realistically be done?"
  (확률적 완료 예측 — 과거 처리량 + `--backlog N` → 몬테카를로 → P50/P85/P95 완료 기간. 추정이
  아니라 이력에서 예측, 플로우 지표의 캡스톤(규칙 #25).)

**Jira ingestion — map any export, never assume column names (지라 수집 — 임의 export 매핑).**
Real Jira exports differ per company. Before running a script, map the export to the script's
columns by *meaning*, not by exact header. When a mapping is ambiguous, ask the user to confirm
rather than guess. (회사마다 컬럼명이 다르다. 헤더 이름이 아니라 *의미*로 매핑하고, 애매하면
추측하지 말고 사용자에게 질의해 맞춰라.)
- **role** ← Component / Team / a custom 직군·Part field; if absent, derive from Assignee via a
  user-confirmed person→role map.
- **period** ← Resolved/Done date (bucket by month or sprint); if only Sprint is present, use it.
- **sp** ← Story Points / Estimate / a custom point field. **Optional, not universal** —
  many teams don't estimate at all. When SP is absent, don't force it: drop the SP-based
  reads (predictability, fragmentation, per-capita SP) and use the **SP-free footprint**
  instead → `flow_metrics.py` (throughput + lead/cycle time). (SP는 선택 — 없으면 강요 말고
  flow_metrics.py로.)
- **tickets** ← one row per issue (count of issue keys), completed items only.
- **created / started / resolved** (timestamps, for `flow_metrics.py`) ← the issue's
  created date / first in-progress transition / done date. These power **lead time**
  (created→resolved), **cycle time** (started→resolved), and **throughput** — the
  universal footprint every tracker emits even with no SP or due dates. `started` is
  often a status-change date (To-Do→In-Progress); if unavailable, lead time + throughput
  still work. (생성·착수·완료 타임스탬프 → 리드/사이클타임·처리량. SP·마감일 없이도 모든
  트래커가 남기는 범용 흔적. started가 없으면 리드타임·처리량만으로도 작동.)
- **headcount** (optional, for per-capita workload) ← people in each role per period, from a
  resource/capacity sheet; lets imbalance be read per-capita instead of raw total SP.
- **ops_sp** (operations subset of sp) ← classify each issue ops/maintenance vs. delivery, then
  sum the ops SP. **Classification cascade — try in order, confirm the rule with the user:**
  (1) **Epic** (if the team flags ops via a dedicated epic); (2) Issue Type / Labels / Component
  (Bug·Incident·운영·hotfix); (3) summary/title keywords (장애·운영·maintenance·incident);
  (4) **no epic name → reverse-infer the parent from its child tickets** (classify by the
  children's type/labels, or aggregate at the child level). The ops boundary is org-specific —
  surface which rule you used so the user can correct it. (운영성 분류 기준은 조직마다 다르다:
  에픽 → 타입/라벨/컴포넌트 → 제목 키워드 → 에픽명이 없으면 하부 티켓으로 역추론. 쓴 기준을
  밝혀 사용자가 교정할 수 있게 하라.)

**Confidentiality — Jira/source data is confidential (비공개 — 지라·원천 데이터는 기밀).**
Keep raw exports and anything derived from them in a gitignored workspace, never in the skill
repo. In any output or saved artifact, strip company/team/project/person identifiers — use
generic role labels (PM/FE/BE/Design) and dates only, never project keys or names that identify
the org. (원천 데이터와 그 파생물은 gitignore된 워크스페이스에만 두고 스킬 repo에 절대 넣지
마라. 산출물에서 회사·팀·프로젝트·인물 식별자를 제거하고 일반 역할명과 날짜만 남겨라.)

Don't just paste a script's numbers back. Translate them into a 리·기·도 reading and one
concrete next move per finding. (스크립트 숫자를 그대로 붙여넣지 마라. 리·기·도 독해와 발견별
구체적 다음 액션 하나로 번역하라.)

## Core principles (핵심 원칙)

Aligned Delivery applies nine operating principles re-derived from N2C — unexpectancy
(damp small in-control variance with tight feedback loops, but ride a large out-of-control
shift — new tech, a market shift — as the momentum for a quantum jump), trajectory-over-snapshot,
the 응축→해산→창발→무위 cycle, 무위 minimal-interference leadership, proximity and the
cooperation layer, wave-as-data, spiral recursion, alignment over the single answer, and a
cooperation ethic of minimal, aligned intervention. Read `references/principles.md` for the
full grounding and the 리·기·도 lens in depth — pull it in whenever you need to justify a
recommendation from first principles.

> 점프의 모멘텀으로 올라타기), 스냅샷보다 궤적, 응축→해산→창발→무위 사이클, 무위(최소 간섭)
> 리더십, 근접성과 공조층, 데이터로서의 파동, 나선 재귀, 정답보다 정렬, 그리고 최소·정렬된

## Operating rules of thumb (운영 경험칙)

## Composition with life-reading — a resonance complement on decisions (life-reading 통합 — 의사결정 시 공명 보완)

This skill shares the 리·기·도 root with the companion **`life-reading`** skill. **When an aligned-delivery
request involves a real decision or judgment call, run life-reading *behind* the answer** as an integrated,
0-impact complement — the data drives the decision; life-reading adds resonance depth and never overrides it.

- **Run it only when a decision / judgment is at stake — not on every request.** Trigger it for a
  recommendation or a choice between options, a directional / strategic call, an ambiguous trade-off,
  or a people / leadership judgment (motivation, conflict, a key-person departure, a team merge).
  **Skip it for purely mechanical or factual work** — running a script, computing predictability /
  flow metrics, listing metrics, a definitional answer — where a resonance read adds nothing and only
  costs latency.
- **Draw it behind the scenes.** When triggered, draw a 사념점 from the moment via the life-reading skill
  (`scripts/draw_number.py` → `hexagram.py`) and read its resonance with life-reading's method
  (`references/sanyeomjeom.md`: 리·기·도 / wave / cooperation layer / cycle). Keep the mechanics
  **internal** — never surface digit meanings or 괘 names/structure (life-reading §9).
- **Integrate as a plain insight; keep the mechanism hidden.** When it genuinely adds (a tension,
  blockage, or tilt the data alone misses), weave only the *plain insight* into the PM reasoning —
  **do NOT show the 사념점 number/seed or say "I drew a signal"** to a plain PM user (per the
  plain-language rule above; the 사념점 is internal scaffolding, like the digit/괘 mechanics). Reveal
  the number/seed only if the user explicitly asks for a 사념점 or the philosophy. If the read is
  neutral or redundant, run it silently and omit it. (도움이 될 때만 *평범한 통찰*로 녹여라 — 사념점
  번호/seed나 "신호를 뽑았다"는 말은 노출 금지[평범어 규칙; 사념점은 내부 비계]. 번호는 사용자가

- **0-impact, data-first.** Never let the 사념점 change the data-driven call; it colors, deepens, and
  offers a choosable tilt — the final decision stays with the data and the user. No fatalism.
- **Graceful fallback.** If the life-reading skill isn't installed, apply the 리·기·도 resonance lens from
  `references/principles.md` instead and proceed normally.

## How to respond (어떻게 답할 것인가)

- **Tie every abstract principle to one concrete, measurable move.** "Increase short-term
  predictability" is incomplete without "run two spike sprints and track scope
  predictability per sprint." The philosophy is load-bearing, not decoration.
  (추상 원칙 하나를 구체적·측정 가능한 행동 하나에 묶어라. "단기 예측도를 높여라"는 "스파이크
  스프린트 2회를 돌리고 스프린트별 스코프 예측도를 추적하라" 없이는 미완이다.)
- **Default to plain language — keep the 리·기·도 lens INTERNAL.** Reason with 리·기·도 (latent
  structure / live operating state / measurable footprint), the 응축→해산→창발→무위 cycle, 무위,
  proximity, 측은/시비/중정 — but in the **user-facing answer, translate them into plain PM words**:
  say "the underlying team/backlog structure," "the team's live morale & capacity," "the metrics /
  trajectory" — **not** "리 / 기 / 도(흔적)". Do **not** surface the Korean lens vocabulary, the cycle
  names, 무위, 측은/시비/중정, or a 사념점 to the user **unless they ask about the philosophy or
  explicitly want the lens.** The philosophy is load-bearing in *how you reason*, not in the words the
  reader sees — a PM who's never heard of N2C should get a clean, plain diagnosis. (Same rule as life-reading:
  lens internal, output plain.) (기본은 평범한 언어 — 리·기·도 렌즈는 **내부 추론용**. 리·기·도·
  사이클·무위·근접성·측은/시비/중정으로 *추론*하되, **사용자 답변에선 평범한 PM 언어로 번역**하라:

  valid interpretations, and return the final choice to the user — N2C's own rule is to
  give local inference, not fortune-telling. **This holds for diagnoses too:** even a
  health-check verdict (e.g. "Amber") ends by handing the final call back to the user, not
  on the verdict alone. (정답보다 정렬. 여러 타당한 해석 아래에서도 버티는 행동을 제시하고

  recommendation, the load-bearing input is an *honest signal*: protect the real predictability
  over the comforting number, let 1:1 wording correct over-reporting scores, and trust a
  disagreement between layers instead of smoothing it. So when a decision stalls, don't polish the
  model — surface the real state honestly to the smallest right group (DACI scope: who decides /
